On-Air Sound
Off by default = studio-clean. When on, every song, break, and ad gets a very subtle "tuned-in FM" colour: a gentle softening of the highest frequencies and a slight band-limit, the way a real broadcast sounds versus a clean studio file. It is deliberately understated — on good speakers you may not hear any difference at all; it is most noticeable on small or phone speakers. Toggling it does not change the track that is playing now: it takes effect on the next few tracks as the buffered queue cycles through, so give it a moment before you judge the change.
