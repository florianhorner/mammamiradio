## Going forward

SUBJECT_TOO_LONG dominates — 72 of 110 failures. The pattern is "stuff every detail into the headline so the merge commit reads like a changelog entry." The fix is subject discipline: keep the headline scannable (`feat(scope): one verb phrase`), push the bullet list of what changed into the body. The new commit-msg hook fails locally on >72-char subjects, so the next instance won't make it into history.
